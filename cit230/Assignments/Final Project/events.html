Biking the Continental Divide

Grab your helmet and cycle on over to the 27th Annual “Cycle the Continent Divide!” Mark your calendars for June 9-16! This biking excursion will take you over 500 miles of varying terrain Bozeman, Montana to Plentywood, Montana. Basic requirements and supplies include:

  • Mountain Bike (At least 5 gears)
  • Navigation Device
  • Camping gear (Tent, camp stove, sleeping bag, etc.)
  • Expert biking ability
  • Food and Water for at least 5 days

The cost is $75 dollars per person or $350 for a group of 5+. The more people you bring, the more you save!

This event has been around for almost as long as we have! 5 days of cycling along a beautiful mountain range. You'll get to see everything from cool mountain streams, high treetops, and incredible cliffs and mountainsides. People from all over travel here to bike the route and see what everyone in biking dreams of seeing. It's a trip of a lifetime that you don't want to miss!
