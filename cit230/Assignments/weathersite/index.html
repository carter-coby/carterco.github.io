Franklin Breaks Record

Yesterday, Franklin nearly went back into the winter. At 10:37 PM broke the all-time cool temperature record in the month of June of 51°F by managing to get a very-cool temperature of just 49°F. Residents of the city mentioned that they were surprised that it was the coldest it had ever been during this time of the year.
